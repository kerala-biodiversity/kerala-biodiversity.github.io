Evening with Dr. S D Biju

Sep 1, 2024

Sathyabhama Das Biju, born in May 1963, is a renowned Indian amphibian biologist and wildlife conservationist, often referred to as the “Frogman of India.” He heads the Systematics Lab at the University of Delhi’s Department of Environmental Studies and is recognized for his significant contributions to amphibian research, including the discovery of over 100 new species and two new families of amphibians. Biju has received numerous accolades for his work, including the Sanctuary Wildlife Service Award and the Kerala Sree Award. His passion for amphibians and dedication to conservation have made him a leading figure in the field.

Join us for an engaging program,

Event : "Talk with Dr. S D Biju"

Date : September 5, 2024, at 4 PM

Venue : Sahitya Akademi Hall, Thrissur.

Everyone is welcome to attend this insightful discussion with Sathyabhama Das Biju, where he will share his experiences and knowledge about amphibian conservation and research. Don’t miss this opportunity to learn from one of the world’s foremost amphibian experts!
